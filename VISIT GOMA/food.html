Best Restaurants in Goma

From lakeside dining to authentic local spots here are the top rated restaurants in Goma according to real traveller reviews.

1

Lac Kivu Lodge

European $$ – $$$
⭐ 4.5 (66 reviews)

Lakeside dining right on the shores of Lake Kivu. Expansive menu with fish, shrimp, chicken and more with a breathtaking view to match.

TripAdvisor Google Maps
2

Il Rasentino

Italian, Pizza $$ – $$$
⭐ 4.5 (19 reviews)

Authentic Italian in the heart of Goma carbonara, amatriciana, fresh pasta and wood-fired pizza. Not cheap, but widely considered the best Italian in the city.

TripAdvisor Google Maps
3

Le Chalet

Pizza, Bar $$ – $$$
⭐ 4.1 (78 reviews)

The most reviewed restaurant in Goma. Wood-fired pizza, lakeside garden, kayak rentals and a Sunday buffet. A local institution.

TripAdvisor Google Maps
4

Salt and Pepper

Indian, Asian $$ – $$$
⭐ 4.1 (42 reviews)

Indian, Chinese and Congolese food all under one roof. Good portions, Wi-Fi available, and great value at $5–10 per meal.

TripAdvisor Google Maps
5

Le Volcan Resto & Pub

Italian, Indian $$ – $$$
⭐ 4.6 (8 reviews)

Fine dining meets pub atmosphere. Great for groups and a festive night out Italian and Indian dishes in a lively setting.

TripAdvisor Google Maps
6

Pili Pili

Indian, International $
⭐ 4.3 (12 reviews)

Affordable and lively great Indian food, Congolese dishes, and a nightclub atmosphere. The most budget-friendly spot on this list.

TripAdvisor Google Maps
7

Le Petit Chalet

Café, American $$ – $$$
⭐ 4.7 (9 reviews)

Breathtaking views of Lake Kivu, fresh juices and a relaxed café vibe. A hidden gem with the highest rating on this list.
